Your Account

From this section, you can manage all information on this site connected to your PostgreSQL community account. Other than your basic profile information, this includes news and events, professional services and comments. Note that most of the data you submit to this site needs to be approved by a moderator before it's published.

To submit a new record, or to edit an existing record, select the type of record in the menu on the left.

Permissions model

News, Events, Products and Professional Services are attached to an organization. One or more persons can be given permissions to manage the data for an organization. If you do not have permissions for an organization and think you should have, you need to contact the current managers for that organization. You can find a list of organizations and managers here.

Migrated data

For most of the data migrated from the old website has unfortunately not been connected to the proper organizations and accounts. If you have any data submitted that is not properly connected to you (most likely this will be your account not being connected to the proper organization(s)), please contact webmaster@postgresql.org and let us know which objects to connect together.
